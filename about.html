Our History

After the death of his son Theodore, creative director Alex Sabour decided to leave the agency he was employed at to pursue other ambitions. The need to have a company to still work on freelance projects lead to the creation of Gorilla Media in December 2016. A few months later, Alex approached a small 2-man production company called CustomFellas, consisting of video director Sasan Askari and producer Lelo, Shalby to collaborate on what would become one of the biggest danish viral videos of all time: #IAmDanish. The meteoric success of #IamDanish led to a string of other award winning social good campaigns including Face Your Brother, Face Your Sister and #BlandDig.

However, good fortune was cut short once again when Alex lost his second son Adam in 2018.

Despite the challenges Gorilla was facing, the team still able to design and execute the largest global anti-revenge porn campaign entitled #AskFirst for Danish Women's Society and PornHub, as well as, continue to win more awards including the highly prized Rambukken.

The continued success of Gorilla, even during this turbulent time, was able to convince Danish Investors GrowYou to inject capital into the company securing not only better frames, but also the talent needed to take Gorilla to the next level.

With 150+ videos, and 3000% growth in less than 3 years Gorilla Media has proven not only to be a competent team, but also a unique agency that pushes the boundaries of what is possible in the realm of advertising and video.

Values

Creativity. Integrity. Performance. Courage.

Creativity: we believe that the budget of a campaign should never dictate the quality of the ideas that are pitched. Creativity doesn’t require a budget. Great ideas are always great, and great ideas demand attention from the world - and they will not be ignored.

Integrity: we conduct our business in accordance with the highest standards of professional behaviour and ethics. Our business is built on sustainable relationships, and founded on trust.

Performance: We intend to be an industry leader wherever we compete. We are committed to excellence and quality. Our people are empowered to exercise initiative and are accountable for their results.

Courage: We are bold. Our ideas are bold. We take initiative and entertain the controversial ideas. We are not afraid of and will not be deterred by the fear of negative reactions. We learn from our mistakes and perceive them as opportunities.
